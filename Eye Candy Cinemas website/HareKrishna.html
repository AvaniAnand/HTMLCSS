HARE KRISHNA! is a documentary on the life of Prabhupada the 70-year-old Indian Swami who arrived in America without support or money in the turbulent 1960s. It explores how he ignited a spiritual revolution in consciousness, known worldwide as the Hare Krishna Movement.
